The Tall And Short Of It

Jason & Sarah - October 7, 2018 - Grounds for Sculpture

Hi! We're getting married!

We picked the fall because it's the best season, and we picked Grounds for Sculpture because we had a fun date there once. This place has everything: sculptures of people, modernist sculptures that might represent people, and people looking at sculptures. And peacocks!

Both the ceremony and reception will be at Rat's Restaurant on the Grounds. It's named after the character in The Wind in the Willows, looks like a Monet painting, and is filled with fireplaces, so it's everything Sarah could have ever wanted in either a venue or place to live forever. It's only zoned for one of those, so wedding it is.
