Getting There


About Grounds for Sculpture

We have been visiting Grounds for Sculpture for years because it's pretty cool. The grounds cover more than 42 acres and are filled with amazing sculptures. We think you should probably show early before the ceremony (or even the day before) so that you have time to see everything they have to offer!

train plane car

From Philadelphia

By car: Take Interstate 95 North, across Scudders Falls Bridge (over Delaware River), to Interstate 295 South to Exit 65B/Sloan Avenue West. After exit stay in right traffic lane for 2/10ths of a mile, following signs for Grounds For Sculpture, and take the jughandle to cross over Sloan Ave. Go through the traffic light onto Klockner Rd. Take Klockner Rd. to next traffic light and turn right onto East State St. Extension. Go 8/10ths of a mile and make second left turn onto Sculptors Way. Go 2/10ths of a mile and turn left into the main entrance of Grounds For Sculpture. Travel time is approximately 45 minutes.

From New York

By train: From New York (Penn Station), take NJ Transit to the Hamilton stop (stop closest to Grounds For Sculpture). Taxi ride is 5 minutes to Grounds For Sculpture. You may also choose to take NJ Transit bus #608 from the Hamilton Train Station. Check http://www.njtransit.com for up-to-date schedules.

By car: Take Interstate 95 North, across Scudders Falls Bridge (over Delaware River), to Interstate 295 South to Exit 65B/Sloan Avenue West. From Cleveland

By car: The best way to drive to the venue is to take I80/I76 past Philadelphia. Luckily, you'll run into some Wawa's (ask Sarah if you don't know why this is important) to keep yourself fueled on the trip.
